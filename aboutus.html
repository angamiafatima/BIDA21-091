About Us

The BIG Picture Pty Ltd is a company with limited liability incorporated in accordance with the company laws of Botswana.

To many, the word “movies” is synonymous with the name “The BIG Picture”; the biggest movie exhibitor by far (more than double the size of it’s nearest competitor).

The BIG Picture owns around 60-65% of the market, with the largest 3D footprint in this territory.

Our History

The BIG Pictures journey through Botswana- the cinema experience today is one that is truly immersive with crystal clear visuals and sound that reverberate in your soul.
